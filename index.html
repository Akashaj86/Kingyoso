About Kingyoso

KINGYOSO is a Neosoul/Funk/Blues band native to Richmond, Virginia.

Members:

  • Lead Vocals/Guitar/Songwriter - Goldfish
  • Backing Vocals/Bass - Sammy P Stiles
  • Guitar - Corey Bertsch
  • Sax - Chet Frierson
  • Trombone - Alex Powers
  • Ukelele - Stu Kindle

Influences: Esperanza Spalding, Sister Sparrow and The Dirty Birds, Hiatus Kaiyote, Kat Wright, Lakestreet Dive and Etta James

Kingyoso has previously performed at venues such as: The Dark Room, Lickinghole Creek Craft Brewery, The Camel
